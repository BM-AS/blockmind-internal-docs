---
title: What Elite is
description: Product-level explanation of the Elite experience and why it exists.
---

Elite is the part of BlockMind that feels like a persistent operator instead of a one-shot chatbot. A user starts from the platform, but the real work happens in that user's runtime and workspace, which gives the system continuity across sessions and tasks.

From a product point of view, the promise is simple: BlockMind is not just answering questions, it is running work on the user's behalf and streaming progress back into the product.

## Why it exists

- **Continuity**: a user's work does not reset every time they open chat.
- **Operational depth**: the runtime can execute tools, produce artifacts, and report progress as the work unfolds.
- **Clear system ownership**: the platform coordinates, the runtime executes, and the operating workflows provision and maintain the environment.

## What the user experiences

1. The user starts a chat or task from the platform.
2. The platform routes that work into the Elite runtime.
3. The runtime emits structured progress and completion events back to the platform.
4. The UI shows live progress rather than waiting for one big final response.
