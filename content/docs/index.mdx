BlockMind has three layers that matter in practice: the product surface users touch, the Elite runtime that does the work, and the operational workflows that keep workspaces healthy. This site explains those layers in that order so non-engineers can stop early and engineers can keep drilling down.

The goal is progressive disclosure. Each page starts with the shortest truthful explanation first, then adds the implementation detail needed to debug, extend, or review the system.
